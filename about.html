ABOUT ME.

Eniola is a signed BETH model based in Nigeria, and works mainly on beauty, portrait works, fashion editorials and commercial print jobs. Raised in Lagos, she fell in love with modelling after competing at a school pageant that she won a category, and has been doing it professionally ever since. She has worked with different photographers and makeup artists, has done campaigns and worked with companies including CasualBecky and others. Eniola aims to keep following her heart and trust where fate leads her with the goals of expanding her modeling career, and continuing to explore the fashion world.
